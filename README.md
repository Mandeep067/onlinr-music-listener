# Online Music Listener

A web-based music streaming platform designed for three user roles: **Admin**, **Artist**, and **Listener**. The platform allows artists to upload music, listeners to stream songs, and admins to manage users, content, and platform operations.

---

## Features

### ** Admin**
- Manage all users (artists & listeners)
- Approve or reject artist registrations
- Remove or update any uploaded song
- Control platform settings

### ** Artist**
- Upload new songs with metadata
- View, edit, or delete their uploaded music
- Track song plays (if analytics added)
- Manage their profile

### ** Listener**
- Stream songs in real-time
- Browse by artist, genre, and categories
- Create playlists (future enhancement)
- Like or favorite songs
- Search for music instantly
